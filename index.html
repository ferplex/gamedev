// about nautilus lab

Local AI.
Zero gatekeeping.

Nautilus Lab is a one-person indie studio based in Tlaquepaque, Jalisco, México. Every tool, bot, and asset pack runs on local hardware — an HP t740 thin client running Fedora with an AMD Vega iGPU, Ollama, and InvokeAI.

We don't depend on cloud APIs or expensive subscriptions. That means no usage limits, no watermarks, no monthly bills — and tools that keep working offline.

The philosophy is simple: build in the open, share what works, collaborate honestly. If a tool we made helps your studio ship faster, that's the point.
